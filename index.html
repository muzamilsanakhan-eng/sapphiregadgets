Brand Protection

MAP Compliance &
Pricing Integrity

Pricing integrity is the #1 concern brand managers raise when evaluating a distribution partner. We've built our entire operation around it — non-negotiable, no exceptions.

Every listing we manage, every channel we sell through, and every marketplace we participate in adheres to the manufacturer's minimum advertised price — without exception, without negotiation, and without workarounds.

We do not engage in grey-market purchasing, unauthorized repricing, or channel tactics that would compromise your brand's value perception. When Sapphire Gadgets sells your product, your customer sees the brand experience you've worked to build.

Discuss Brand Protection

MAP Policy Enforcement

Real-time listing monitoring across every marketplace. Pricing discrepancies are corrected immediately — before they damage your brand.

Catalog & Listing Accuracy

Product titles, images, and descriptions maintained exactly as specified by the brand. No unauthorized modifications, ever.

Inventory Traceability

Every unit purchased through authorized channels with full documentation. Chain-of-custody records available to any compliance team on request.

Customer Experience Standards

Fulfillment, packaging, and customer service that reflects your brand's quality. Ratings actively monitored to protect your marketplace reputation.

No Unauthorized Channels

We sell exclusively through channels authorized by each brand partner. No grey importers, discount platforms, or unauthorized resellers.

Partnership Process

How Brand
Partnerships Work

Our partnership onboarding is designed to give brand managers confidence that Sapphire Gadgets operates with the commercial discipline and operational accountability that serious distribution relationships require.

01

Brand Application & Review

Submit a brand application outlining your products, pricing policies, and distribution requirements. We review your marketplace presence and MAP compliance framework.

02

Introductory Meeting & Operations Audit

We schedule a partnership meeting to understand your brand's goals, review your distribution structure, and demonstrate how Sapphire Gadgets aligns with your standards.

03

04

Launch & Ongoing Growth

We place first purchase orders, activate listings, and begin ongoing partnership management — with regular performance reporting and proactive communication throughout.
