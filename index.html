Brand Protection

MAP Compliance &
Pricing Integrity

Brand managers and supplier approval teams tell us that pricing integrity is their #1 concern when evaluating a new distribution partner. We take that responsibility seriously — and have built our entire operation around it.

Our MAP compliance policy is non-negotiable: every listing we manage, every channel we sell through, and every marketplace we participate in adheres to the manufacturer's minimum advertised price — without exception, without negotiation, and without workarounds.

We do not engage in grey-market purchasing, unauthorized repricing, or channel tactics that would compromise your brand's value perception. When Nova Orvian sells your product, your customer sees the brand experience you've worked to build.

Discuss Brand Protection

MAP Policy Enforcement

We monitor all our listings in real time across every marketplace to ensure full compliance with your MAP policy. If a discrepancy is ever identified, it is corrected immediately — not negotiated around.

Catalog & Listing Accuracy

Product titles, images, descriptions, and attributes are maintained exactly as specified by the brand. We do not modify brand content, create unauthorized variations, or publish listings that deviate from your catalog standards.

Inventory Traceability

Every unit of product we carry is purchased through authorized channels with full documentation. We maintain complete chain-of-custody records and can demonstrate purchase provenance to any brand manager or compliance team upon request.

Customer Experience Standards

Our fulfillment, packaging, and customer service standards reflect the quality your brand represents. Customer feedback and ratings are actively monitored to ensure your brand's marketplace reputation is consistently protected.

No Unauthorized Channels

We sell exclusively through channels authorized by each brand we partner with. We do not sell through unauthorized resellers, grey importers, or discount platforms that would undermine your pricing structure or brand positioning.

Transparent Reporting

Brand partners have access to regular performance reporting covering sales velocity, channel distribution, pricing adherence, and inventory levels — giving you full visibility into how your products are moving through our network.

Partnership Process

How Brand
Partnerships Work

Our partnership onboarding is designed to give brand managers confidence that Nova Orvian operates with the commercial discipline and operational accountability that serious distribution relationships require. We move carefully, communicate clearly, and structure every agreement around the brand's long-term interests.

01

Brand Application & Review

You submit a brand application outlining your products, pricing policies, and distribution requirements. We review your brand's existing marketplace presence and MAP compliance framework before proceeding.

02

Introductory Meeting & Operations Audit

We schedule a partnership meeting to understand your brand's goals, review your current distribution structure, and demonstrate how Nova Orvian's operations align with your standards. Full operational transparency from day one.

03

Distribution Agreement Signed

We formalize the partnership through a clear distribution agreement that outlines pricing policies, authorized channels, catalog standards, reporting cadence, and mutual obligations — protecting both parties' interests.

04

Launch & Ongoing Growth

We place our first purchase orders, prepare inventory for channel distribution, activate listings, and begin the ongoing partnership management process — with regular performance reporting and proactive communication throughout.
