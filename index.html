Brand Protection

MAP Compliance &
Pricing Integrity

Pricing integrity is consistently cited as the #1 concern among brand managers evaluating new distribution partners — and it is the commitment we have built our entire operation around.

Our MAP compliance policy is non-negotiable: every listing we manage, every channel we sell through, and every marketplace we participate in adheres to the manufacturer's minimum advertised price — without exception, without negotiation, and without workarounds.

We do not engage in grey-market purchasing, unauthorized repricing, or channel tactics that compromise your brand's value perception. When Sapphire Gadgets sells your product, your customer sees the brand experience you've worked to build.

Discuss Brand Protection

MAP Policy Enforcement

We monitor all our listings in real time across every marketplace to ensure full compliance with your MAP policy. If a discrepancy is ever identified, it is corrected immediately — not negotiated around.

Catalog & Listing Accuracy

Product titles, images, descriptions, and attributes are maintained exactly as specified by the brand. We do not modify brand content, create unauthorized variations, or publish listings that deviate from your catalog standards.

Inventory Traceability

Every unit we carry is purchased through authorized channels with full documentation. We maintain complete chain-of-custody records and can demonstrate purchase provenance to any brand manager or compliance team upon request.

Customer Experience Standards

Our fulfillment, packaging, and customer service standards reflect the quality your brand represents. Customer feedback and ratings are actively monitored to ensure your brand's marketplace reputation is consistently protected.

No Unauthorized Channels

We sell exclusively through channels authorized by each brand we partner with. We do not sell through unauthorized resellers, grey importers, or discount platforms that would undermine your pricing structure or brand positioning.

Transparent Reporting

Brand partners receive monthly performance reports covering sales velocity by channel, pricing compliance logs, inventory levels, and marketplace account health metrics — delivered on a fixed schedule without needing to request them.

Partnership Process

How Brand
Partnerships Work

Our partnership onboarding is designed to give brand managers confidence that Sapphire Gadgets operates with the commercial discipline and operational accountability that serious distribution relationships require. We move carefully, communicate clearly, and structure every agreement around the brand's long-term interests.

01

Brand Application & Review

You submit a brand application outlining your products, pricing policies, and distribution requirements. We review your brand's existing marketplace presence and MAP compliance framework before proceeding.

02

Introductory Meeting & Operations Audit

We schedule a partnership meeting to understand your brand's goals and demonstrate how Sapphire Gadgets' operations align with your standards. We also assess channel saturation risk to ensure we can add distribution value without creating marketplace conflicts with your existing partners.

03

Distribution Agreement Signed

Our standard distribution agreement covers authorized channel scope, MAP enforcement obligations, catalog compliance standards, monthly reporting cadence, minimum purchase commitments, and mutual termination terms — protecting both parties' commercial interests from day one.

04

Launch & Ongoing Growth

We place our first purchase orders, prepare inventory for channel distribution, activate listings, and begin ongoing partnership management — with regular performance reporting and proactive communication throughout. Typical onboarding: 5–10 business days.
